Yocto Notes
-------------

To search for a recipe:
    bitbake-layers show-recipes <recipe>

To search for a variable in a recipe:
    bitbake -e <recipe> | grep <variable>
example:
    bitbake -e gdcfonts | grep ^WORKDIR=

To clear a recipe:
    bitbake -c cleanall <recipe>
example:
    bitbake -c cleanall gdcfonts

To bake only one recipe:
    bitbake <recipe>
exampe:
    bitbake gdcfonts
